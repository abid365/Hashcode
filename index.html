Hey, coding is not easy! You may get lost easily.

So here are some useful resources that might help you :

freecodecamp :

FreeCodeCamp is a non-profit organization that offers free coding classes and certification in web development. It was founded in 2014 by Quincy Larson, a software developer and teacher. The organization's mission is to provide a high-quality education in computer science to anyone who wants to learn, regardless of their income or background.
Here's the link : freecodecamp

geeks for geeks :

GeeksforGeeks offers a variety of online resources to help people learn and improve their programming skills, including articles, tutorials, and practice problems. The site covers a wide range of topics, including algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and computer science concepts. In addition to the educational resources, GeeksforGeeks also has a community forum where users can ask for help, share their work, and get feedback from other users and experts.
Here's the link : geeks for geeks

css tricks :

CSS Tricks is a website that provides resources and tutorials for web developers and designers. It was founded in 2007 by Chris Coyier, a web developer and designer. The site covers a wide range of topics related to web development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and design principles.
Here's the link : css tricks

PY4E :

Python for Everybody (Py4e) is a free online course that teaches the Python programming language. It was created by Dr. Charles Severance, a Professor in the School of Information at the University of Michigan. The course is designed to be self-paced, so students can learn at their own pace and on their own schedule.
Here's the link : PY4E
